The HFT Pipeline: A Nanosecond Race

High-Frequency Trading is a sequential race against time, where each component shaves off precious nanoseconds. The following diagram illustrates the complete, end-to-end flow of an HFT system, from the moment market data arrives to the final execution of a trade.
